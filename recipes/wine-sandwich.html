The best sandwich i've ever ate

Wined Ham Sandwich made by my girlfriend Angie for me.

This sandwich was made for me in one of the first days onf June 2022, that made rethink my love for sandwiches at that moment

This recipe should take you no more than 15minutes and is very noob friendly

    Ingredents

  • Wined Ham
  • Sandwich bread Orowheat (this is my bread choice, but you can use what you like or have)
  • Mayonaisse
  • Ketchup
  • Avocado
  • Mexican Oaxaca Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce (if u like it)

    Steps

  1. First you need to put the bread on the pan at low heat for aprox 5 to 10 minutes
  2. At the same time we have to string the cheese and cut the tomato in fine rings
  3. Then we should cut little pieces of avocado
  4. When the bread is a little toasted we should add the ham and cheese so it can heat up and gratin
  5. Finally we remove the bread with the ham and cheese and put it on a plate, then add the mayo, ketchup, tomato and the avocado
  6. And voila! Theres your delicious Wined Ham Sandwich
