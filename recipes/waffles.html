Classic Waffles for a good breakfast

In this recipe we will be making some waffles, a nice and simple recipe even I can make (I think)

    Ingredents

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking pouder
  • 2 tablespoons fo white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups of warm milk
  • One third of melted cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

    Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately
  4. Enjoy with your favourite topping!
