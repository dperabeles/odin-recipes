A really great smoothie for starting your day

In this recipe we will be doing an banana and strawberry smoothie, with optional oats for getting your daily fiber

    Ingredents

  • Bananas
  • 5 or 6 strawberries
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • Milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vainilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Ice Ice Baby
  • A blender

    Steps

  1. First we will add 1 or 2 bananas on the blender
  2. Then add the strawberries
  3. The add sugar at your own taste
  4. Add 5 to 8 spoons of oats
  5. Then add 1 teaspoon of vainilla
  6. Next we will add a little of cinnamon
  7. This steps is optional but i recommend on adding a little of ice for that smoothie feeling
  8. Now make of of your blender and get it nice and thick smoothie
  9. Last step is to enjoy!
